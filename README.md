## QMS Modules to Explore

### 1. In-Process Quality
- **Deviation Management**: Tracks deviations from standard operating procedures
- **CAPA (Corrective and Preventive Actions)**: Manages corrective and preventive measures

### 2. In-Product Quality
- **Product Complaints**: Handles customer complaints about products
- **Recall Management**: Manages product recalls
- **Adverse Event Reporting**: Reports and tracks adverse events

### 3. QMS Management
- **Supplier Management**: Manages supplier quality and compliance
